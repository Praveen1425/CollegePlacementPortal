About Mock Interviews

Mock interviews help students prepare for real placement interviews by simulating the interview environment and providing constructive feedback on their performance.

Benefits:
  • Builds confidence in handling interview questions
  • Helps identify areas for improvement
  • Familiarizes students with interview formats
  • Reduces anxiety for actual placement interviews
Tips for Effective Mocks
  • Assign interviewers with relevant domain expertise
  • Schedule at least one week before actual interviews
  • Provide detailed feedback for improvement
  • Focus on both technical skills and communication
  • Structure the mock similar to company interview formats
